About HID

Hope in Darkness Club (HID) is a student-led initiative founded at Noida Institute of Engineering and Technology.

What We Do?

Peer Support

We believe in the power of sharing. Our platform encourages open dialogues about mental health.

Tranquil Touch

Workshops to nurture personal development, equipping individuals with tools to manage stress.

Embrace

A safe space for members to connect and uplift one another through peer-led groups.

Psychologist

Meet Mr. Naman Jain

Naman Jain is a Psychologist with a Master’s degree in Clinical Psychology. He works with students and individuals who are navigating emotional difficulties, stress, relationships, and personal challenges. His approach to therapy is gentle, compassionate, and reflective, drawing from Psychodynamic Therapy and Dialectical Behaviour Therapy (DBT). He offers a safe and non-judgmental space where students can explore their feelings, better understand themselves, and learn healthier ways to cope and grow.
