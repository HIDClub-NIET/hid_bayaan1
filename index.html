About HID

Hope in Darkness Club (HID) is a student-led initiative founded at Noida Institute of Engineering and Technology. We are dedicated to addressing the often overlooked aspects of mental health and personal development. Our mission is to break the silence, challenge stigmas, and create a safe space where everyone can openly discuss their mental well-being. We believe that healing begins with sharing our stories and embracing the challenges we face together.

What We Do?

Peer Support Group

At HID Club, we believe in the power of sharing. Our platform encourages open dialogues about mental health, helping to reduce stigma and foster community understanding.

Tranquil Touch

Our workshops nurture personal development, equipping individuals with tools to manage stress and enhance mental health through mindfulness and self-care strategies.

Embrace

We create a safe space for members to connect and uplift one another. Through peer-led groups and activities, we build a community rooted in empathy and understanding.
